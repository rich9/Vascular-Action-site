Vascular Action, LLC is specialized in the treatment and care of dialysis vascular access.

Our mission is to provide the best dialysis access care to every patient with advanced renal failure.

Through our commitment to excellence, we will obtain the trust of our patients and their healthcare providers.

Staff and Physicians

Dr.Leclercq

Dr. Leclercq is a Board Certified Nephrologist and Internist. After graduating with honors from the University of Louvain in Brussels, Belgium, he began his medical career in private family practice and pediatrics. He then pursued his long term goal of studying nephrology, completing his residency at the University of Miami. Soon after, he became a member of the medical research team at the prestigious Harvard University.

He later returned to the University of Miami as a leading member of their research team. Dr. Leclercq has over 20 years of experience and is an entrepreneur in his field. He refined his practice by performing interventional procedures on dialysis vascular access patients and brings his highly skilled expertise to the patients of the greater Tampa Bay area. He and his team have successfully performed more than 10,000 procedures and continue to do so with Vascular Action in Dialysis Vascular Access Care.
